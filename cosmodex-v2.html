I want this habit because I value…
Pick 1–2 values. Research: values-aligned habits survive mood and motivation dips (Self-Determination Theory).
Doing this makes me…
Identity framing beats outcome framing. "I am a runner" beats "I want to run a marathon." (James Clear, +32% adherence.)
What's the 30-second version?
BJ Fogg: the habit must be doable in 30 seconds on your worst day. You can scale up later.

When will you do it?
Event-based anchors beat time-based reminders for automaticity (Stawarz et al.).
